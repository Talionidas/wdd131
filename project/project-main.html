Biomes

Promenade of the Condemned

First Biome

There is always a small portion that is charged with Traps, don't rush it and use the roll midair to dodge the spikeball (you are invulnerable even midair during a roll) Be careful with the Dual wielder since they always teleport to you after spotting you

Ramparts

Second Biome

Always be aware of where the ground is, fall damage can quickly become a problem. I recommend first clearing in a straight vertical line, then doing the horizontal towers to minimize confusion

Black Bridge

First Boss Biome / Third Biome

There are 3 things to lookout for: A ground wave attack, a leap attack and a damaging barrier. Indications are in order: Arm backwards charging, crouch position, Sound and red particles

Stilt Village

Fourth Biome

There are spawners that will keep throwing eggs at you once they gain awareness of you, take them out as fast as possible. Pirate Captains can also be a pain due to their canon projectile, i'd recommend a shield or ranged kill

Clock Tower

Fifth Biome

Need the Clock Tower key to advance to the next Biome. Lookout for Throwers and assasins (invisible until you reach their platform, then do a 2 stage dash attack)

Clock Room

Second Boss Biome / Sixth Biome

There are 2 Attacks to look out for: A chain grab that will stun you (vertical straight attack) and a shuriken throw in all direction. Second phase shown by speech bubble and shurken throw. Otherwise straightforward fight.

High Peak Castle

Seventh Biome

You need 2 Miniboss keys to advance to the next Biome, which can be found in the colored doors, The Minibosses are always the same. Be careful about horizontal and vertical attacks from throwers and spear soldiers (they can pierce walls).

Throne Room

Third Boss Biome / Eighth Biome

This Bossfight is a lot easier with a shield, since the boss is quite slow and does big attacks. He summons 2 elites and 3 normal spawns between phases. Biggest attack to look out for is his charge that pushes the player to the spikes
