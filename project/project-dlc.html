DLC Biomes

Dilapidated Arboretum

Second Biome

When you arrive into the Biome you have a left and a right part, I recommend first going vertical to get the portals for emergency exits, and then exploring horizontaly later on. Throwers and Flowers are all over.

Morass of the Banished

Third Biome

This biome is made horizontaly, with 2 minibosses each time you reach the lowest floor (main attacks are front and back swipes, and a lunge). Watch out for the dart range attacks and blood spawns

Nest

First Boss Biome / Fourth Biome

This Boss has 2 phases, an active and a passive one, during the passive you will be bombarded with attacks from below, just go into a corner and dodge into the wall. For the Active phase there will be close attacks one long swipe and a projectile attack.

Fractured Shrines

Fifth Biome

Keep your eyes open, this Biome is all about fall and Trap damage. The only threat here are the minibosses that open a vault, but they are optional as they only give you a legendary weapon and some gold.

Undying Shores

Sixth Biome

This Biome is horizontaly downwards, with 2 small vertical parts midways and at the end. Be careful to not jump to your death. Prioritize the Spawners, as they revive the dead if left unchecked and hide behind Walls

Mausoleum

Second Boss Biome / Seventh Biome

Straightforward first phase, second phase he will hang in the air, use the mushrooms on the sides if you have a melee build, Turrets will struggle to do anything.

Dracula's Castle

Eigth Biome

Dracula makes an appearance every other Minute and either makes a minion into an elite, drops a swarm of bats or makes your camera go upside down. You need a Key from Medusa on the bottom right side to go to the next Biome.

Master's Keep

Third Boss Biome / Ninth Biome

This Boss has two stages: The first one is mainly a spam of projectiles, a shield will make short work here, otherwise keep your distance and dodge. The second phase is a bit trickier, turrets are worthless here as they cannot track the Boss, and Shield don't work for half the attacks.
